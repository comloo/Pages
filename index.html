Monopoly

I realized a lot of people complain about monopoly being too long, so I decided to create a 'mini' version of monopoly, for those who want to play monopoly, but don't have to play it for 10 hours in order to finish one game.

The functionality and gameplay are still the same as the original, but small alterations were made such as the board size, making the game go by faster.

Created in Java.

Plat Engine

Inspired by the Steam Engine, I decided to created something similar. A database application to store video game information and statistics.

This was challenging to make, even without all the original graphics I had to create/implement, there were a lot of algorithms I had to utilize in order for the database to function. Overall I am happy with the result.

Made in Java.

This Website

Even though it was very time consuming, I learned a lot more about HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript than before. It was worth it.
